# English

## Overview

**Project Heartone** is a comprehensive world-building project designed for tabletop RPG campaigns. Set in a dark medieval fantasy world, it draws inspiration from properties like Runeterra (League of Legends), Dark Souls, The Witcher, and Hollow Knight.

The world teeters on the edge of destruction, caught between ancient natural forces and an encroaching cosmic horror known as **The Blight**.

## Core Concept

> *"The balance of the world depends on the battle between the light of life and the consuming darkness. In between, mortals struggle â€” some to save, others to destroy."*

### The Central Conflict

- **The Heart-Eaters** â€” Cosmic entities born from crystallized negative emotions during an ancient cataclysm called *The Eternal Eclipse*
- **The Natural Bastion** â€” Primal Spirits that protect the world, now weakening
- **Internal Accelerators** â€” Corrupted individuals within civilizations who unknowingly hasten the world's destruction
- **The Mortal Resistance** â€” Organizations and individuals actively fighting the Blight across civilizations

## Gambaran Umum

Dunia ini berada di ambang kehancuran, terjepit antara kekuatan alam kuno dan kengerian kosmik yang dikenal sebagai **Wabah**.

> *"Keseimbangan dunia bergantung pada pertarungan antara cahaya kehidupan dan kegelapan yang mengonsumsi. Di tengah-tengahnya, manusia berjuang â€” beberapa untuk menyelamatkan, yang lain untuk menghancurkan."*
