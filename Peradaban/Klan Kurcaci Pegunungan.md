## Gambaran Umum

**The Star-Smith Confederacy** adalah konfederasi kurcaci yang mendiami kedalaman Pegunungan Puncak Abadi. Mereka adalah master kombinasi antara ancient rune magic, crystal refinement, dan steam-powered technology yang advanced.

> *"Kami tidak hanya memahat batu - kami memahat bintang menjadi senjata, mengukir rune ke dalam kristal, dan menggerakkan mesin dengan kekuatan kosmos itu sendiri."*

## Culture dan Society

### Values
- **Innovation through Tradition** - New ideas must respect ancient knowledge
- **Independence** - Strong desire to remain autonomous
- **Craftsmanship** - Quality and precision above all
- **Practicality** - Results matter more than ideology

### Social Structure
- **Clan-based** - Loyalty to clan first, Confederacy second
- **Meritocratic** - Skill and achievement determine status
- **Guild-controlled** - Professional guilds regulate trades

### Beliefs
- **The Great Forge** - Cosmic concept of creation through craftsmanship
- **Star-ancestors** - Belief that kurcaci descendants of cosmic smiths
- **Rune-wisdom** - Ancient runes contain fundamental knowledge

---

## Konflik Internal

### Innovation vs Tradition
- **Steamwrights** want expansion and progress
- **Runekeepers** fear loss of ancient knowledge
- **Crystal-miners** focus on profit, neutral in debate

### External Relations
- **Open trade** (Steamwrights) vs **Isolation** (Runekeepers)
- **Military spending** (Stonefist) vs **Economic investment** (Crystalvein)

## Potensi untuk Campaign

### Hook Ideas
- **Crystal shortage** forces Confederacy to seek new mining territories
- **Ancient rune discovery** threatens to change power balance
- **Steamwright rebellion** against traditional leadership
- **External invasion** testing Confederacy defenses
- **Trade negotiation** with Solara for critical resources

### Alliances
- **Players** bisa interpensi Confederacy representatives
- **Mercenary work** protecting caravans or mining operations
- **Diplomatic missions** to establish new trade routes
- **Archaeological expeditions** into deeper mountain regions
